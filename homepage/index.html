/ The Solution

What is Mixta?

Connections

Tour

Take Away

Mixta offers a venue for visitors to explore and learn more about their favorite bands and artists. Mixta will also suggest new bands and artists based on visitors interests.

Mixta stations will suggest and help visitors navigate to related artists, based on their selections, helping them build their own personalized museum experience.

At the end of their experience, visitors will leave with their self-curated playlist of music built over the course of their journey, which can be accessed any time of Spotify.

/ The Problem

Let's Break it down



Visitors at the rock and roll hall of fame lack a way to establish connections between static artifacts and the music they represent.

Our aim is to engage, teach and inspire through the power of rock and roll, using our three pillars of connection, tour, and takeaway.

/ We Overcame

Design and Development Challenges



Designing for the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame required that we provide a solution that integrates into an existing system.

We created and refined a visual identity and accessable design architecture that feels like rock and fits in with the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame's style, while exemplifying our own unique flair.

We used the latest technologies to create a modern and approachable experience designed to enhance to visitor's experience, assembling our technological and industrial components from the ground up.
